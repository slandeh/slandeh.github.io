Whether it's your first tournament or your hundredth, there's a spot for you here. Check out what's coming up and jump in whenever you're ready!

Standard — Regulation H & I

Standard is the main competitive format for the Pokemon Trading Card Game. Only cards from the current legal sets can be used — a great starting point since you won't need every card ever printed! If you're new, don't worry: pre-built theme decks are a totally valid way to get started.

VGC 2025 — Regulation I

Video Game Championships uses the latest mainline Pokemon games. Battles are double battles — you bring 4 Pokemon and lead with 2. New to VGC? A great way to get started is practicing with friends using rental teams in casual play — just note that rental teams are not permitted in Championship Point-earning events like Cups and Challenges, so you'll want your own registered team for those.

Regulation I
Allowed Pokédex
Paldea Pokédex #001–400 Kitakami Pokédex #001–200 Blueberry Pokédex #001–242 (Except Pecharunt) Event or Pokémon HOME
Restrictions
2 Restricted Legendary Pokémon
Pokemon GO — Battle League

GO events are played directly in the Pokemon GO app using GO Battle League rules. Bring your phone and get ready for some fast-paced battles! Championship Point-earning events like Cups and Challenges use Great League only. Ultra League and Master League are available for casual play during open league sessions.

Great League — Competitive Ultra League — Casual only Master League — Casual only
